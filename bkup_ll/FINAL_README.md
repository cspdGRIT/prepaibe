## ğŸ“– Topic Details

### 1. Contract Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** Indian Contract Act 1872
- Formation of contracts (offer, acceptance, consideration)
- Void and voidable contracts
- Doctrines: privity, frustration, quantum meruit
- Breach and remedies
- Bailment and agency
- Specific sections with explanations

### 2. Constitutional Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** Indian Constitution
- Preamble and fundamental principles
- Fundamental Rights (Articles 12-35)
- Directive Principles (Part IV)
- Basic Structure Doctrine
- Judicial Review
- Federal structure, amendments
- Key cases: Kesavananda Bharati, Maneka Gandhi

### 3. Criminal Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** IPC, CrPC, Evidence Act
- Mens rea and actus reus
- Offenses: murder, culpable homicide, theft
- Common intention, abetment
- Criminal procedures: FIR, investigation, trial
- Cognizable vs non-cognizable
- Dying declarations, Test Identification

### 4. Law of Torts (18 cards)
**Covers:** Civil Wrongs
- Negligence, vicarious liability
- Defamation (libel and slander)
- Nuisance, trespass
- Strict liability, absolute liability
- Remedies: damages, injunction
- Key doctrines and maxims

### 5. Property Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** Transfer of Property Act 1882
- Sale, mortgage, lease, gift
- Six types of mortgages
- Doctrines: lis pendens, election, part performance
- Rights of mortgagor and mortgagee
- Rule against perpetuity
- Specific sections explained

### 6. Family Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** Personal Laws
- Hindu Marriage Act: conditions, divorce
- Muslim law: nikah, dower, talaq
- Special Marriage Act
- Succession and inheritance
- Custody and maintenance
- Recent amendments (triple talaq ban)

### 7. Company Law (18 cards)
**Covers:** Companies Act 2013
- Types of companies, formation
- MOA and AOA
- Doctrines: ultra vires, indoor management, lifting veil
- Directors' duties and liabilities
- Meetings and resolutions
- Winding up, NCLT, CSR

### 8. Professional Ethics (16 cards)
**Covers:** Advocates Act, BCI Rules
- Duties to court and client
- Professional misconduct
- Attorney-client privilege
- Advertising restrictions
- Fee regulations
- Disciplinary proceedings
- CLE requirements

### 9. Civil Procedure Code (18 cards)
**Covers:** CPC 1908
- Jurisdiction (territorial, pecuniary, subject matter)
- Res judicata and res sub judice
- Pleadings: plaint, written statement
- Interim injunctions
- Decree, judgment, order
- Execution, appeal, revision

### 10. Indian Evidence Act (18 cards)
**Covers:** Evidence Act 1872
- Relevancy of evidence
- Burden of proof and standard
- Hearsay rule and exceptions
- Dying declaration
- Best evidence rule
- Presumptions and estoppel
- Examination of witnesses

### 11. Labour & Industrial Law (16 cards)
**Covers:** Industrial Laws
- Industrial Disputes Act
- Strike, lockout, closure
- Retrenchment, lay-off
- Trade Unions Act
- Factories Act (health, safety, welfare)
- Minimum Wages Act
- Collective bargaining

### 12. Arbitration & Conciliation (16 cards)
**Covers:** Arbitration Act 1996
- Arbitration agreement and process
- Appointment of arbitrators
- Interim measures
- Arbitral awards
- Setting aside grounds
- Enforcement
- Domestic vs international arbitration
